## Overview

To view patient records in Squbix:

1. Login to your Squbix dashboard.
2. Click on "Patients" in the left sidebar.
3. Use the search bar to find the patient by name or ID.
4. Select the patient's name in the search results.
5. Patient details and medical history will appear.

## Tips

- Access rights depend on your user role.
- Contact admin if you cannot find a patient record.
