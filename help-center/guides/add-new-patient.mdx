## Overview

Register new patients quickly in Squbix.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Patients module">
    Select "Patients" from the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add New'">
    Click the "Add New Patient" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details">
    Fill in required fields: name, birth date, contact information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Save". The patient is now registered.
  </Step>
</Steps>
