---
title: Login Errors
description: Resolve login issues in Squbix.
---

## Symptoms

- "Invalid credentials" message
- Unable to sign in to dashboard

## Causes

- Wrong username or password
- Account locked after failures

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact your admin">
    If still unable to login, ask your system administrator to check your account status.
  </Step>
</Steps>
